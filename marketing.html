How it works

From invite to insight
in three steps

NovaCS strips the friction from structured assessment. Issue a code, let candidates complete on their own time, get weighted results instantly.

🔑

Issue an access code

FAQ

Common questions

How does the candidate access the assessment?+
The recruiter generates a unique access code from their dashboard and sends it to the candidate. The candidate visits the NovaCS portal, enters the code and their phone number to verify identity, and the assessment begins. No account creation required.
Can I customise the scoring weights for each role?+
Yes. From Settings you can assign a percentage weight to each of the four categories. Weights must total 100%. The platform shows a live preview of how any change would affect your current candidate pass/fail outcomes before you save.
What happens if a candidate switches tabs?+
Every navigation away from the assessment window is recorded as a tab-switch flag. The total count appears alongside their result. The flag does not automatically reduce their score — that call is yours — but it is always visible for borderline decisions.
Is there a limit on how many candidates I can assess?+
Starter plans include 20 assessments per month. Professional includes 50. Enterprise is unlimited. If you need a temporary limit increase for a large intake, contact us and we will accommodate you at no extra charge.
Can multiple people on my team use the platform?+
Yes. Admins have full access including settings and billing. Recruiters can issue codes and review results. Viewers can see results but cannot take any action. All seats are managed from the Settings page within your dashboard.
How do I get started?+
Click Get started in the navigation, create your organisation account, and you will be inside your dashboard within two minutes. The first 14 days are free on any plan — no credit card required. Contact us to arrange a guided demo if you prefer.
