How NovaCS works

Three steps from setup to decision. No complex integrations, no training required — your team can be assessing candidates the same day.

01
🏢
Set up your organisation
Request access to NovaCS, create your organisation profile, configure your scoring weights and pass threshold to match the role requirements.
02
🔑
Issue access codes
Invite candidates individually or upload a CSV of applicants to bulk-generate unique, single-use access codes. Codes can be time-limited and revoked at any time.
03
📊
Review and decide
As candidates complete their assessments, scores land in your dashboard automatically. Review weighted scores, category breakdowns, and move candidates through your pipeline.

Platform features

Everything your team needs to assess at scale

A complete toolkit for modern recruitment — from candidate verification through to post-assessment analytics.

⚖️
Configurable Weighted Scoring
Set the weight of each skill category to match role requirements. Adjust pass thresholds per open position. Scores are recalculated automatically.
Live now
🔑
Single-Use Access Codes
Every candidate gets a unique, time-bound code. Issue individually via the dashboard or bulk-upload via CSV. Codes expire on use and can be revoked.
Live now
📊
Batch Analytics
Compare all candidates in a cohort at a glance. View score distributions, pass rates by role, category averages, and completion trends over time.
Live now
👤
Full Candidate Profiles
Each candidate gets a dedicated profile with their weighted score, category breakdown, hiring pipeline stage, recruiter notes and email action templates.
Live now
🛡️
Integrity & Anti-Cheat
Tab-switching is detected and flagged to the recruiter automatically. Single-use codes prevent repeat attempts. Each assessment is time-limited and locked to one device session.
Live now
👥
Multi-Recruiter Teams
Invite your entire hiring team to your organisation. Assign admin, recruiter or viewer permissions. All activity is logged in the audit trail.
