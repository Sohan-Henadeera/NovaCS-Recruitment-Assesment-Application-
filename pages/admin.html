Log in as an organisation's admin to troubleshoot issues on their behalf. All actions taken while impersonating are recorded in the audit log.

⚠️
Use with caution
While impersonating, you will see and be able to act on behalf of that organisation's admin. Every action you take will be attributed to you (NovaCS Admin) in the audit log, not to the organisation's users.
